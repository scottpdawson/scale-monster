What is ScaleMonster?

My daughter and son's piano teacher has given them scale assignments over the past few years and I saw fit to write them down in a notebook all in once place. I thought it'd be cool to create an online interactive version that shows the fingerings for each scale. If you have a scale not listed that you'd like me to add, send me a note on twitter.
